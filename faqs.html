Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is this website about?
This website is a music portal where you can listen, download, and explore various songs, albums, and artists.
Q: Who can use this website?
Both registered and non-registered users can use this website.
Q: What benefits do registered users get?
Registered users can download complete albums, access exclusive content, and receive personalized recommendations.
Q: How do I register?
Click on the "Register" icon on the top-right corner of the page, fill out the registration form, and verify your email address.
Q: What can non-registered users do?
Non-registered users can listen to songs, explore albums and artists, and access limited content.
Q: Can non-registered users download songs?
No, non-registered users cannot download songs. They can only listen to songs online.
Q: What if I face technical issues while using the website?
Please contact our support team through the "Contact Us" link at the bottom of the page.
Q: How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
Please use the "Feedback" form to report bugs or suggest new features.
Q: Do I need to pay to use this website?
No, registration and basic usage are free.
Q: How do I contact the website administrators?
Please use the "Contact Us" link at the bottom of the page to reach out to our support team.
